How InnoHomeTech Aligns with the UN Sustainable Development Goals

InnoHomeTech is built on a vision that goes beyond just smart living — it's about contributing to a better, more inclusive future.

By bringing affordable home automation to everyday households, empowering local talent, and encouraging practical innovation, our business aligns closely with the spirit of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Through our services and impact model, we aim to support progress where it matters most.

Creating Opportunities – SDG 8

One of our main focuses is to open up real job opportunities. We train and involve local technicians, electricians, and students in learning the skills they need to work with smart systems. This doesn’t just help our business grow — it helps people grow too. With hands-on experience and new skillsets, these individuals can earn better and live with more stability.

Encouraging Practical Innovation – SDG 9

At InnoHomeTech, we believe innovation isn’t about flashy products — it’s about solutions that solve real-life problems. We provide systems that help reduce electricity bills, improve home safety, and add convenience to everyday routines. Our goal is to make this technology simple, reliable, and reachable — not complicated or expensive. In this way, we support smarter infrastructure while keeping our focus on usefulness and sustainability.

Bridging the Gap – SDG 10

What truly makes us proud is that we’re giving access to smart living to people who were never part of that circle before. Whether someone lives in a big house or a small one, in a city or village, they deserve comfort and control over their space. By keeping our prices fair and our system easy to use, we’re helping reduce the gap between different income levels — everyone gets a chance to benefit.

At the heart of it, InnoHomeTech is about balance — using business as a way to create value, both for people and the planet. We’re not chasing trends; we’re building something that actually matters.
